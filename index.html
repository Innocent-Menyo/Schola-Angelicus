About Us

At Schola Angelica, we are committed to changing lives through music. Our charity provides underprivileged youth with the opportunity to learn and master a variety of musical instruments, including piano, cello, violin, guitar, and organ.

Our mission is to offer quality music education to children and young adults who face financial barriers to formal education. By providing free training in these instruments, we open doors for youth to explore their musical talents and develop skills that will serve them for life.
